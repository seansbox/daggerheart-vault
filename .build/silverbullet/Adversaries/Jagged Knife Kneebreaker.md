## I’ve Got ‘Em - Passive
Characters Restrained by this adversary take double damage from attacks by other adversaries in the scene. _“They have you pinned and you know that’s bad news.”_

## Hold Them Down - Action
Make an attack against a target in melee. On a success, they take no damage but are Restrained and Vulnerable. A PC can break free by with Strength (12) roll or are let go automatically when Major damage or higher is dealt to the Bruiser. _“They slam you to the ground with unbelievable strength.”_
